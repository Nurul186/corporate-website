Sustainability-Driven Decisions, Ethical Supply Chain

At Loob Holdings, sustainability drives our decisions—especially in our supply chain. We prioritise ethical sourcing, eco-friendly packaging, and efficient logistics to reduce our environmental footprint. By partnering with like-minded suppliers, we build a transparent, responsible supply chain that supports long-term environmental and business resilience.

Case Study & Storytelling

Tealive X HHI Recycling

Our collaboration with Heng Hiap Industries has successfully collected and processed 650 kg of plastic, paper, and mixed materials from pilot outlets, reducing landfill waste and carbon emissions. The materials are repurposed into new products, aligning with our vision of a circular economy.

Tealive Jom Kosong Campaign

In partnership with the Ministry of Domestic Trade and Cost of Living, we launched this initiative to promote sugar-free beverages, donating 20 cents per sale to provide school meals for underprivileged children. Our goal is to raise RM3.5 million within a year.

Plastic cup to Paper cup

By September 2024, we fully transitioned to paper cups, reducing plastic waste by 91%. Plastic usage dropped from 47.82 tonnes in June to just 3.42 tonnes in October, marking a significant step towards sustainability.

Next Generation Self-Destruct Plastic Cup & Straw

Effective 16 January 2026, we are introducing a next-generation self-destruct plastic cup and straw powered by biotransformation technology. This material enables plastic to degrade safely within approximately two years if mismanaged. This innovation complements our broader packaging strategy.

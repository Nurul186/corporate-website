HERE’S THE TEA ABOUT TEALIVE

Tealive is Southeast Asia's leading regional lifestyle tea brand, currently having over 900 outlets across three continents. At present, Tealive offers more than 30 types of beverages, from signature brown sugar pearl milk tea and hand-crafted teas to coffee and smoothies. In addition to Malaysia, Tealive is now present in Vietnam, Myanmar, Brunei, the Philippines, Cambodia, Mauritius, Canada, Singapore, UAE, India and Thailand.
